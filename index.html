FAQ

Q. What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is an event where in participating teams are provided different real-life problems and they have to design a technological solution to anyone of the problems given in the given time limit. The final product developed should be usable in real life.

Q. Can I code before coming to the hackathon?

You can come prepared with half baked codes, write your own APIs and modules.

Q. How long is the hackathon going to last?

The hackathon is a 24-hour event.

Q. What is the team size?

The team can consist of minimum 2 and a maximum of 4 participants.

Q. How much does it cost?

Thanks to our college the hackathon is completely free of cost.

Q. What are the different domains?

AI/ML, Web/App, Security/Blockchain

Q. Is the event going to be online or offline?

The hackathon is going to be an Offline event at our college.

Q. Will there be a mentoring session?

Yes, there will be mentors and industry experts who will guide you during the hackathon.
